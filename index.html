About Us

Dr D Y Patil School of Engineering is one of the most prestigious academic groups in the Pune City and state of Maharashtra which is Approved by AICTE and Affiliated to SP Pune University. DYPSOE was established in 2010 by the Chairman of Ajeenkya D.Y.Patil Group.
